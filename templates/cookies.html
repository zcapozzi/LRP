LacrosseReference PRO: Cookie Policy LR PRO: Cookie Policy
Our cookie policy is pretty straightforward: keep the minimum amount of information possible to maximize the user's experience using the site. In practice, that means that we store a cookie for each device so that we can do 2 things:
  • avoid having to sign-in every time you return to LRP
  • maintain a user cart for those in the process of making a purchase
Things that we do not do with cookies:
  • Leverage external parties to access your browsing history to serve ads (because we do not serve ads)
  • Share your browsing history on LRP with anyone (I focus on building a product you want, not on monetizing your attention)
  • Whatever other gray-area things some sites do with cookies and browsing history
The cookies that we store have no personally identifying information in them. They are just randomly generated numbers and characters that are linked to you only inside our database (i.e. even if someone got a hold of your cookie information, they wouldn't be able to connect it to you).
I trust my customers not to abuse my guarantee policy, and I want to earn your trust by being open about what I do with cookies AND by making sure those things are not shady.
